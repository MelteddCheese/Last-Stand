Use 'a' 'w' 's' 'd' or the arrow keys to move the player

Use mouse to aim at the enemies and click the button to shoot

Kill the fast moving zombie 3 times to replenish the player's life and the bullets in the weapons.

Use the weapons panel to switch between the weapons and to activate mines

Traps placed can capture 4 zombies before disapparing

Press Spacebar for activating the Jetpack

Click ENTER to start the game
